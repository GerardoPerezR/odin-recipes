Grog

Grog is a bla bla bla

Ingredients:

  • 1 ounce of Navy rum
  • 3 ounces of water
  • 1/2 ounce of freshly squeezed lemon or lime
  • 3 tablespoons of honey

Steps

Bring the water to a boil Turn off the heat, and add the rest of the ingredients Stir until it reaches a smooth texture Pour into a heat-safe mug.
